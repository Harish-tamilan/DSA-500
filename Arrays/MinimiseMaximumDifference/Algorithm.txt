Method-1 O(nlogn) time

1. Sort the input array
2. Assign tempMin to arr[0]+k and tempMax to arr[n-1]-k
3. Assign sum to arr[n-1]+k - tempMin
4. Traverse the array from 0th index to n-2 index
5. On each traversal, compare ith index+k arr[i]+k with tempMax and assign the greatest 
    to max
6. Similarly, compare i+1th index-k arr[i+1]-k with tempMin and assign the least to min
7. If max-min<sum, reassign sum to max-min
8. Return sum
